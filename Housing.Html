Patterson Hall

Address: 612 Main St, Columbia, SC

Type: Coed Suite-Style

Overview: Patterson offers suite-style living with 2–4 students per suite, shared bathroom, and community lounges on each floor.

Student Reviews:

  • “Super social, lots of first-years.”
  • “Rooms are cozy, but the location is great for walking to classes.”

Bates House

Address: 123 Bates Dr, Columbia, SC

Type: Coed Traditional

Overview: Traditional-style dorm with hall bathrooms, strong community vibe, and easy access to dining.

Student Reviews:

  • “Feels like classic freshman dorm life.”
  • “Bathrooms get busy, but the community is worth it.”

Capstone

Address: 902 Barnwell St, Columbia, SC

Type: Coed Suite-Style High-Rise

Overview: High-rise tower with suite-style rooms, great views, and the famous Capstone dining at the top.

Student Reviews:

  • “Love the views from the upper floors.”
